UC Berkeley Ultracold Atomic Physics Research

I worked on what is labeled project e8 under PI Dr. Dan-Stamperkurn and Postdoc Kayleigh Cassillas. With this incredible team I learned the basics of vacuum systems, baking parts to remove particles/contaminations, how control systems execute experiments with precise timing, the structure of atoms (Ti in particular), what a Zeeman slower is/works and the list goes on.

UC Riverside Ultracold Molecules Research

This was my first summer research and I cannot be thankful enough. This experience was where my fate was cemented due to how much I learned and the intellectual stiumulatiomn constructed by the group. Here I learned about Artiq, a well known AMO control system, constructing printed circuit boards and soldering components onto them and then watching these circuits come to life, aligning optics for Raman spectroscopy, and a good foundation of molecular physics.
